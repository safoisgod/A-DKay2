Book Spotlight: Killed by Illiteracy

“Killed by Illiteracy” is a gripping exploration of how illiteracy shapes individuals and societies. Through poignant storytelling and real-life examples, the book unveils the harsh realities faced by those deprived of education.

Written by Kojo Ampem Daako, this book provides an urgent call to action for addressing illiteracy's far-reaching consequences and its impact on personal and societal development. A must-read for anyone who cares about social change and education.
